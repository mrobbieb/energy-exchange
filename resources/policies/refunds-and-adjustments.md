# Refunds and Adjustments

## Refund Eligibility
Refunds are evaluated based on:
- Transaction status
- Delivery amount
- Cancellation timing
- Failure reason

## Types of Adjustments
Adjustments may include:
- Full point refunds
- Partial point refunds
- Prorated host credits
- Fee retention

## Processing Time
Refunds and adjustments are typically processed shortly after transaction resolution but may be delayed for investigation.

## Platform Discretion
The platform reserves discretion in issuing adjustments based on available data and system integrity.
