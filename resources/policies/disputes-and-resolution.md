# Disputes and Resolution

## Opening a Dispute
A dispute may be opened if a user believes:
- Energy delivery was misreported
- Points were incorrectly credited or debited
- The transaction outcome was incorrect

## Review Process
During dispute review, the platform may examine:
- Transaction logs
- Meter readings
- User acknowledgments
- Station or operator input

## Outcomes
Dispute outcomes may include:
- No adjustment
- Partial adjustment
- Full adjustment
- Account warnings

## Final Decision
Dispute decisions are final once issued.
Repeated disputes may affect account privileges.
