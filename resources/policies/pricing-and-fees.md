# Pricing and Fees

## Pricing Structure
Pricing may be expressed as:
- Points per kWh
- Fixed point totals
- Time-based or demand-based adjustments

Displayed pricing reflects current conditions at the time of booking.

## Platform Fees
The platform may charge:
- Transaction fees
- Service or processing fees
- Station coordination fees

Fees are disclosed prior to confirmation whenever possible.

## Station Fees
Some stations or locations may impose additional fees, which are set by the station operator and not controlled by the platform.

## Dynamic Pricing
Pricing may change based on:
- Energy demand
- Time of day
- Location constraints
- Promotional incentives

Confirmed transfers are not affected by later pricing changes.
