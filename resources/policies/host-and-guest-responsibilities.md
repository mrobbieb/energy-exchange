# Host and Guest Responsibilities

## Host Responsibilities
Hosts are responsible for:
- Providing advertised energy
- Maintaining compatible equipment
- Following safety and station rules
- Cancelling listings if energy becomes unavailable

## Guest Responsibilities
Guests are responsible for:
- Ensuring their system can accept the transfer
- Arriving within the agreed time window
- Following station and safety requirements

## Shared Responsibility
Both parties share responsibility for:
- Clear communication
- Safe operation
- Confirming transfer completion when required
